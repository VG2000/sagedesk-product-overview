The Challenge

Information Overload

Hundreds of reports, bulletins, and publications to track across ONS, GOV.UK, think tanks, and media

⏱

Manual Preparation

Hours spent drafting briefings, preparing for questions, and researching policy positions

🔗

Disconnected Tools

Separate systems for research, writing, data, media monitoring, and communications

Frame the problem: MPs and their teams are overwhelmed. SageDesk solves this by bringing everything into one place.

Resource Library

  • Searchable, filterable library of reports, articles, podcasts, and policy documents from trusted publishers
  • Every resource is automatically summarised by AI into short briefings and comprehensive analyses
  • Generate sceptical analyses that reframe claims and challenge assumptions from an alternative perspective
  • Subscribe to customised resource summaries filtered by format, source, publisher, or topic and be notified by email
  • An integrated AI research assistant that allows you to ask questions in plain English and receive sourced, evidence-based answers from your document library, filtered as needed
  • Scope queries to a single resource for deep-dive analysis in a specific document

Constituency Data Explorer

  • Detailed constituency profiles for all 650 seats currently covering economy, deprivation, health, child poverty, food banks, local authorities, and house prices
  • Employment and earnings data with sector-by-sector breakdowns sourced from Nomis, APS, and BRES
  • Index of Multiple Deprivation scores, domain breakdowns, and LSOA decile distribution charts from IMD 2025
  • Health indicators including life expectancy, disease prevalence, mortality, and childhood obesity from OHID Fingertips and NHS Digital
  • Child poverty rates, food bank parcel data with historical trends from Trussell Trust, and local authority budget breakdowns
  • House prices by property type and buyer type with 30-year price and sales volume trend charts from HM Land Registry
  • Fully extensible and customisable — can be expanded to encompass any number of constituency-level datasets

System Dynamics Modeller

  • Build, calibrate, and simulate System Dynamics models entirely in the browser — stocks, flows, variables, and parameters on an interactive canvas
  • Real-time formula validation, automated error checking, and RK45/Euler integration with interactive D3.js time-series charts
  • Calibrate models against real-world CSV data with Nelder-Mead optimisation, and run sensitivity analysis with fan charts
  • Sub-model boundaries for isolating subsystems, dimensional analysis for unit consistency, and full JSON import/export
